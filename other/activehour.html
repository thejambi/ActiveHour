About the weather dot: this option uses your location — but it is never accessed while the option is turned off. It shows a dot at the minute mark matching the temperature (Fahrenheit). For example, 80° shows an orange dot at the 20-minute mark; 30° shows a dot at the bottom of the face. Below 0° or above 60° the dot turns a noticeable color.
